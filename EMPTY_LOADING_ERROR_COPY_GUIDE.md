# Empty/Loading/Error Copy Guide

Use this copy structure on all pages.

## Empty state template

- **What happened:** "No matching places yet."
- **What to do next:** "Try relaxing one or more filters."
- **Action:** one clear CTA (example: "Clear Filters")

## Loading state template

- **What happened:** "Loading your places..."
- **What to do next:** optional short hint (example: "This can take a few seconds on larger lists.")
- Keep layout stable while loading (no jumping content).

## Error state template

- **What happened:** short factual message (example: "We could not load this list.")
- **What to do next:** one practical instruction (example: "Check connection and retry.")
- **Action:** one clear CTA (example: "Retry")

## Tone rules

- Use plain language.
- Keep to 1-2 short sentences before CTA.
- Avoid blame or technical jargon.
- Prefer action-focused verbs: `Retry`, `Refresh`, `Clear`, `Open`.

## Reusable examples

### Empty

- "No nearby places found for these filters."
- "Try increasing radius or turning off Indoor-only."
- CTA: `Reset Filters`

### Loading

- "Building suggestions from your preferences..."

### Error

- "We could not refresh suggestions right now."
- "Try again in a moment."
- CTA: `Retry`
