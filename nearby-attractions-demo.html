📖 How It Works

1️⃣ Auto-Detection

When you add a new location, nearby attractions are automatically detected based on GPS coordinates. The system searches for nearby locations within the specified radius.

2️⃣ Sorting & Information

Attractions are sorted by distance from your location (closest first). Each attraction shows:

  • 📍 Distance in miles
  • 🚗 Approximate drive time
  • ⭐ Google rating (if available)
  • ✅ Status (In App or Available on Google)

3️⃣ Click to Navigate

If location exists in app: Click "View Details" to navigate to that location card and see more information.
If not in app: Click "View on Google" to see the Google Maps listing, or "Add to App" to quickly add it.
